Description

Spirit of the Island is a beautiful Life Sim Sandbox RPG with Cross-Platform Online COOP between PC, consoles and mobile set on a tropical set of the islands.

There’s an old tradition running in your homeland: to become an adult and complete the coming-of-age ritual, you must go on a journey of discovery. Your adventure begins on a distant island, deep in a tropical Archipelago. Explore the Archipelago, meet the locals, and lend them a hand to restore the tourist paradise… while you try to uncover the mysteries of your past!

A PLETHORA OF ACTIVITIES

There’s plenty to do in Spirit of the Island! No matter how you choose to spend your time, you will gain experience and improve your skills, unlock new recipes, explore, do animal husbandry, explore nearby islands, and become more efficient at everything you do, which will make your life simulation easier.

Spirit of the Island features 10 unique skills, such as farming, mining, foraging, social, crafting, and even fishing, which comes as its own special mini-game. Will you become a true master of all?

VAST TROPICAL ARCHIPELAGO

Travel across 14 unique explorable islands, each with its own fauna, secrets, and dangers. Information on who - or what - inhabits these islands has been lost to history, but you will find mysterious caves guarded by ancient creatures — are you brave enough to explore them and uncover the mysteries within? Doing so will net you neat rewards and help you proceed towards your final goal: revealing the secret of your past.

YOUR OWN PLAYGROUND

Spirit of the Island is all about you and what you want to do. The world is your playground and you are free to do anything you want. Go on adventures, manage your lands, renovate, unlock achievements, and immerse yourself in the vast tropical paradise of SOTI. Or begin your journey on a different location: enjoy a new island with more space to unlock the full potential of your imagination but at the cost of a more challenging start. A perfect way to freshen up things for seasoned players.

Character customization is at your service if you decided to change your personality as you play and there is no need to start everything from the beginning.

THE POWER OF TWO

One of the main features of Spirit of the Island is a 2-player cooperative mode, where you can do… everything together! All single-player mode features are available in online play, even the story! Do farming, grow crops and make delicious dishes, build a store and sell your produce to tourists, explore the vast Archipelago to find treasure, and discover the secrets of the tropical paradise together, no matter the platform or place, as Spirit of the Island features full crossplay between PC, Consoles and, starting form the end of Q1 2024, Mobile.

A HISTORIC COLLECTION

Everything you stumble upon on your journey across the Archipelago is a relic of its own kind. Treasures that you find when going treasure hunting, various insects, various fish, even the new plants you grow — it’s all a reminder of the amazing journey and your achievements. All these little bits and pieces are collected in the Museum: a place of wonder and magnificence. Will you find and unlock everything that the Archipelago has to offer and complete your ultimate collection?

Spirit of the Island is set to provide you with a unique and completely cross-platform mix of a farming sim and sandboxed adventure. And our DLC — Adventureland — will expand on the idea even more.
