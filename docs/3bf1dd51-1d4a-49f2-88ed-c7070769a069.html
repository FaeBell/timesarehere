Description

There is an old saying in this country. Whoever passes through the gate will have the power to destroy everything.
Time has passed, and few people remember this legend.
This is the story of a sentry in a certain country.He is a new recruit who has just arrived at his post. But fate is not waiting for him.
Another country, having heard the old lore, has sent an army in search of the “power to destroy everything” that has been passed down through the generations in this country.He must take on a great destiny to protect this country.
To save the people of his country, he must pass through the gate, gain power, and fight the enemy army.
But...Power comes at a price.
Still young, he passed through the gate without hesitation to save his people, but he did not know that.

You will be the new recruit, and you will go through two worlds, the world beyond the gate and the world where he exists.
You will defeat the enemy army and save the people.

This world (The world where he is)


There are powers and items you gained in that world and leveled up in this world. The objective is to use them to bury enemy armies and save people who need help. When you save someone, a soul is added to the vessel. There are 8 vessels in total.

That World (The World Beyond the Gate)


The objective is to receive soul points from special inhabitants and convert them into a powerful force. Robots are watching you and will eliminate you if they discover you. You can ask the inhabitants and animals about the world.
Soul points remain the same. Meat points are converted into money. This can be filled up to 8 containers. Soul points are converted into power-ups and meat points are converted into coins to purchase items. It is up to the player how to allocate them.

Between that world and this world


This is where upgrades and item purchases are made using points earned in that world. It is also the only save point in the game.
Some players may visit this place many times.
