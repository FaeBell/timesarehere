Description

Welcome to The Geomatrix, a digital realm where everything is a polygon, including you!

Create, discover and share levels, compete for the best times and discover hidden secrets. Whether you're a triangle, circle or square, each shape has their own unique abilities that dictate how you solve puzzles and clear levels!

-Features-

Using the in-game level editor, you can make all kinds of crazy creations from difficult time trials to complex contraptions! The level editor features:

  • Various simple and complex shapes, that you can combine to create minimalist structures or huge open worlds

  • Lots of functional blocks, such as the automator and area trigger for the engineers out there

  • Many handy tools that streamline the creation process, like the camera zoom adjuster and background blocks

Grab a friend! Any of your maps can be uploaded to the Steam Workshop to allow them to be played in multiplayer!

  • Deep integration with Steam, use a lobby code or simply invite a friend through the Steam friends menu

  • Compete with each other, for the fastest times and best scores, or work together to solve difficult puzzles

  • Cross-platform compatibility, whether you're playing on a Windows or Mac system, anyone can join their friends, regardless of their operation system

If you're not as interested in creating your own levels, or just want to experience some pre-packaged levels, I've got you covered!

  • A handful of demo levels, to preview the capabilities of the level editor and to test your skills

  • Hidden collectibles and secrets, for the keen-eyed and curious

  • A subtle storyline, of course, for the theorists out there
