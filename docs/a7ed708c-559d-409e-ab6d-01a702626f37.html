Description

Blush Blush is a Dating Game tossed with an Idle Game. It features a light hearted story where the player must rescue a series of fun and attractive anime-boys, who have all been cursed with a dark spell. A curse that can only be broken with the power of love, naturally.

The gameplay is easy! The player must choose how they spend their time wisely, by working various jobs, earning stat points, and arranging dates and gifts for their love interests. A mix of strategy and story, the player will gradually become an accomplished dater, and have the chance to woo more than a dozen characters with more planned for the future.

“So, to turn me back to normal, you just need to hang out with me? That’s cool, I guess. Maybe you could start some sort of curse-based dating service?”

Nimh is a respectful, caring, rabbit-hearted guy who’s interested in everything you have to say. The more you get to know this hunnybunny, the less you’ll be able to resist loving him. He’s just so dang cute!

~~~

“I didn’t ask for your help. But if you want to waste your time bothering me, I guess I can’t stop you.”

Independent and introverted, Volks is the lone wolf type. Literally. He may be closed off and defensive, but that’s just because he’s waiting for someone who cares enough to break down his walls! Deep down, he has a warm and loyal heart.

~~~

“Very well. Change me back, if you can. I shall not bite the hand that feeds me... At least not without asking permission.”

For all your supernatural fantasy needs, Stirling is a suave master of darkness. As you work to lift his curse, you’re sure to discover the eternally torturous kind of love only an immortal can offer. Plus… his outfits sparkle!

~~~

“Have you ever thought about following someone home, quietly figuring out every detail of their life's schedule, and slowly integrating yourself into it to gain their attention? Yeah, me neither.”

Intense, focused, and kind of obsessed with you, Cole is nothing if not observant. Very observant. Like, “how does he know that?” levels of observant. Luckily, that means he knows all the best ways to thrill and delight you! As long as you’re willing to ignore those claws of his…

~~~

“There sure are a lot of weirdos around here. That's cool though. I myself am strange and unusual.”

With hair as dark as ebony and skin as white as snow, Poe is hauntingly beautiful and ready to accept his magpie curse with as much grace as… well… a magpie. Bond over sadness and poetry as you find a way to return Poe to his proper form: the emo dreamboat we’ve all either wanted or wanted to be.

~~~

“Are ya busy? I was thinkin' about hackin' into the power grid and causin' massive blackouts for the lulz. But we could go get ice cream instead, I suppose.”

There’s something about the anti-hero that has us in a permanent chokehold. Enter Anon, the super-intelligent, socially reclusive hacker. Yes, he may have been turned into a lemur, but that’s fine. No one knows who you really are on the internet.
