Description



As the golden age of the World of Twelve draws to a close, a new era is about to begin.
Be your own legend and attempt to make your mark on history! Will you be strong enough to challenge the powerful Ogrest, the monster responsible for all cataclysms and a destroyer of heroes?
From the hit series of the same name, WAKFU will immerse you in a unique fantasy world. Join the millions of other players exploring a world undergoing reconstruction and find your place in the community!


From Foggernaut robotized saboteurs to treasure-hunting Enutrofs, and from pyromaniac Rogues to Xelor masters of time, you're sure to find the character that's just right for you in the 18 unique classes. Each class has unique spells and specialties, which evolve depending on how you play!


Plan your strategy and work with your teammates to defeat the most insidious creatures in the World of Twelve. Your character has many characteristics, and it's up to you to choose your path to become the strongest of all and earn the most loot at the end of each fight.




Have amazing adventures, explore a hundred unique dungeons and successfully complete hundreds of quest that will give you a chance to experience a unique adventure in which you are the main protagonist.


Join 4 million players, form alliances, acquire land and build your “Haven World” to turn into your secret base and develop it as you want.


Defeating the most powerful monsters is not the only way to become a legend! Become a citizen of one of 4 nations and rise to the upper echelons of politics. Master a craft and control the economy… The choice is yours alone!
