Description

Survive the world's deadliest psychopaths. Experience a unique 2 player co-op experience that puts your teamwork and communication to the test. Complete your duties alone or with a partner in a prison like no other.



Nestled in the Pacific Ocean lies the Corton Island Institute of Rehabilitation. Hidden from the public, the island prison harbors society's most notorious criminals, many wielding supernatural abilities.


Join forces with a companion to tackle the job together. Each person will find themselves in separated rooms and must rely only on communication through walkie talkies to complete duties together. No partner? The job can be done entirely solo as well!

Note: The co-op experience relies on the use of voice chat as a vital mechanic.




Take on the role of administration while alone in an office. Fulfill duties to meet your employer's expectations. Failure is not an option, where consequences are as unforgiving as the ocean that surrounds.


Each day, the prison experiences electrical outages. Defend yourself against a diverse range of convicts, all the while ensuring the execution of your duties.




Infiltrate prison network systems and solve puzzles to discover the truth behind how the prison came to be.
